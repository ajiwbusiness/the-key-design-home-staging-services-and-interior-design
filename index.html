Good to know

Questions homeowners ask

How much does working with an interior designer cost?

It depends on scope and finish level. Single rooms typically start in the low five figures; full-home projects scale from there. After your free consultation you'll get an itemized proposal with a fixed investment, so you know the exact number before committing to anything.

Is my project too small to hire a designer?

Almost never. A lot of our favorite work is a single room or a focused refresh — smaller spaces are where thoughtful design makes the biggest difference. If a full-service engagement isn't the right fit, our E-Design option gives you a designer-made plan to install yourself.

How long does a project take?

A single room is typically 6–10 weeks; a full-home design and install runs 4–9 months depending on scope, renovation, and lead times. Your proposal includes a timeline with milestones, and we keep you updated at each one.

Do you only design, or do you handle everything?

We're full-service. We design it, source and order every piece, coordinate the trades, and install and style the finished room. You make the decisions; we handle the logistics, deliveries, and the day-to-day so you don't have to.

What areas do you serve?

We work throughout Salt Lake City and the surrounding Utah area for full-service projects, and we take on E-Design clients anywhere in the U.S. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask — we'll let you know right away.

What happens on the free consultation?

It's a relaxed 30-minute call (or visit) to hear about your space, your style, and your budget, and to walk you through how we'd approach it. You'll leave with a clear sense of next steps and cost — and zero obligation to continue.
